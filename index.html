About Me

I'm a fourth-year automotive engineering student at Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST), graduating in September 2026. My work bridges theoretical control systems with practical automotive applications.

My current focus is developing intelligent EV routing and energy management systems, combining Python for algorithmic design with MATLAB/Simulink for control validation. I've also conducted research into emerging materials, specifically the shape memory effects in graphene foam structures.

I'm driven by hands-on engineering—from CAD design to FEA simulation to web application deployment. I believe in taking full ownership of projects and delivering tangible results that matter.
